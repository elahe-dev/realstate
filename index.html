What we invest in

We source hundreds of opportunities annually and select a small subset for investing across Western Europe and North America. Our sourcing gives us access to some of the better risk-adjusted return opportunities across the alternative landscape with a strong focus on key thematic trends shaping various industries.

Direct Private Equity

We invest in buy & builds, middle market to upper middle market buy-outs, corporate carve-outs and special situation opportunities where enterprise values fall between US$250-2.5bn. This in our view is where we find the best risk-reward opportunities

Direct Real Estate

We invest across the entire real estate capital stack across industrial, residential and commercial opporunities in the sector with a thematic approach across areas of bridge financing, planning plays, income generation, value-add and greenfield / brownfield development
