Defining the spirit of true partnership

We understand that investing is a team sport. We strive hard to innovate and deliver on our promises to our partners so that we are always the capital of choice for our sponsor partners. We realise that the value of our market place is driven by the hard work that our sponsor partners put in to bring an opportunity to Accrete Capital and its community as well as the effort it takes to create value over the duration of an investment.

Why grow your AUM with Accrete Capital

Permanent Capital

Whilst we have investment horizons and the discipline of creating value over a defined period; given our secondary market exchange which provides liquidity options to our community of investors we can be patient with respect to our eventual exit - as a result we do not take myopic views and short-termism in many of our strategic decision makings with our sponsor partners.

Guaranteed execution

Accrete Capital’s backstop fascility allows us to pre-fund transactions up to US$350mn and place it on our balance sheet before marketing it on our platform.

Flexibility

While we have launched with real estate and private equity, we are continuously searching for the right partners to bring new opportunities to our community.

Follow-on capital

We always have a view to support our partners over the long term and see our success in theirs. As such, follow-on capital for other transactions we support becomes incrementally easier for many of our sponsor partners.

Helping you scale

We work with a network of institutional investor that we can bring into transactions that are slightly higher than our average investment tickets. We work closely with our sponsor partners to structure the right kind of capital partnerships for our portfolios.

Strategic Partnerships

Strategic long term revolving capital

Agreements with select originators where Accrete provides a certain amount of revolving capital agreement that gives originators support to grow their business. In turn, Accrete gets a minor interest in the originator and co-investment rights.

Simple deal capital

Agreements with select originators where Accrete provides a non-binding capital agreement, but the agreement highlights a move to change our relationship from simple to strategic one over time.
