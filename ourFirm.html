Our vision is simple ...

To bridge income inequality by making alternative investing more accessible and affordable enabling investors access to opportunities earlier in their life-time and thereby allowing them to compound a much higher return over their entire lifetime.

We are always humbled by the trust that our investor community places with us when they invest their capital on our platform. We make sure to always undertake quality underwriting in order to deliver remarkable risk-adjusted investment returns to our investor community. Harnessing the trust of our investor community allows us to continue to innovate and bring even stronger risk-adjusted investment opportunities to our network; driving a virtuous cycle. We make it our mission to harness the power of this virtuous cycle on our unique platform.

Accrete Capital’s Manifesto

Labour has always been taxed linearly whilst capital has always enjoyed the power of compounding returns. Unfortunately, more and more policies have been supportive of capital as opposed to labor. Over time, the disparity of wealth has become more visible in society where opportunities to invest capital in some of the best asset classes have been made available to only a few ultra high net worths and institutions.

Through the power of technology and the strenghth of our investor community we are looking to empower the masses on their journey of wealth creation; removing barriers, increasing transparency and alignmnet whilst reducing the fee burden that has been challenging the alternative investment industry as of late.

At Accrete Capital, our mission rests on the principal of mutual support of people coming together to achieve more and thereby levelling the playing field.

Meet our leadership team

Lleadership defines culture and likewise culture defines organisations and there is not better predictor of a company’s future than its culture

Ali Shekofti

Founding Partner and CEO

Prior to building Accrete Capital, Ali was an an advisor to Alvarium Capital Partners - a merchant bank forming part of a US$21bn AUM multi-family offices investing in and seeding alternative asset managers predominantly in real estate. Prior to Alvarium, Ali was a founding principal of a frontier market focused venture holding entity investing in e-commerce, ride-hailing, digital classifieds, food delivery and digital media - where he was also a co-CEO of the holding entities e-commerce vertical. Prior to his entrepenreurial life, Ali was also a director of M&A and corporate development at a large pharmaceutical where he concluded close to US$500mn in life science transactions. Ali also had a career in financial services as part of Golman Sachs Financial Institutions Group culminating in him being an independent advisor to many financial sponsor firms in secondaries private equity where he worked on many transactions and private placement fund mandates. Ali is a member of the Young President’s Organisation and went to school at the American University of Sharjah and London Business School.

Arya Bolurfrushan

Founding Partner

Arya was part of Goldman Sachs’ investment strategies group, the think tank behind key asset allocation decisions. As GM & CFO of RAK Petroleum, Arya led an upstream oil and gas roll-up growing it 4x culminating in an IPO (RAKP.OL). Arya is a founding partner of Accrete Capital and serves on multiple boards. Arya is a graduate of Carnegie Mellon and a Harvard Business School MBA. He was named WEF 30 under 30 and is a member of the Young President’s Organisation.

Philipp Triebel

Founding Partner

Philipp was a member of Goldman Sachs’ Special Situations Group - investing the bank’s own capital across asset classes with focus on lower middle market private equity. Philipp founded & built an education start-up from 0 to 150+ employees in the US. Philipp is a founding partner of Accrete Capital, a private equity sponsor. He is a graduate of Harvard Business School MBA program.
